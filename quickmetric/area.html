Definition of Area

Area is the measure of the space occupied by a two-dimensional shape, expressed in square units (e.g., square meters, square feet). It quantifies how much surface a shape covers.

Common formulas for calculating area include:

  • Rectangle: Area = Length × Width
  • Square: Area = Side²
  • Triangle: Area = ½ × Base × Height
  • Circle: Area = π × Radius²

Understanding area is essential for applications in fields such as land measurement, construction, and design.

Units of Area

Square Feet

A square foot is a unit of area commonly used in real estate. It is defined as the area of a square with sides of one foot.

Acre

An acre is commonly used to measure land area. It is equivalent to 43,560 square feet or 4,047 square meters.

Hectare

The hectare is a metric unit for land measurement. It equals 10,000 square meters or 2.471 acres.

Square Mile

A square mile is a large unit of area often used to measure large geographical regions.

Square Meter

The square meter is the SI unit of area, used for measuring smaller plots of land.

Square Yard

A square yard is a unit of area equal to 9 square feet, commonly used in the U.S. and U.K.

Square Inch

A square inch is used in the U.S. and U.K. One square inch equals 6.4516 square centimeters.

Millimeter

The square millimeter is used for very small areas. One square millimeter equals 0.001 square meters.

Faqs of Area Conversion

To convert from Square Inches to Square Millimeters, you can use the conversion factor: 1 Square Inch = 645.16 Square Millimeters.
Formula:
Square Millimeters = Square Inches × 645.16
Example: 5 Square Inches = 5 × 645.16 = 3,225.8 Square Millimeters.

To convert from Square Feet to Square Meters, use the conversion factor: 1 Square Foot = 0.092903 Square Meters.
Formula:
Square Meters = Square Feet × 0.092903
Example: 10 Square Feet = 10 × 0.092903 = 0.92903 Square Meters.

To convert from Square Yards to Square Meters, the conversion factor is: 1 Square Yard = 0.836127 Square Meters.
Formula:
Square Meters = Square Yards × 0.836127
Example: 3 Square Yards = 3 × 0.836127 = 2.508381 Square Meters.

To convert from Acres to Square Meters, use the conversion factor: 1 Acre = 4046.86 Square Meters.
Formula:
Square Meters = Acres × 4046.86
Example: 2 Acres = 2 × 4046.86 = 8093.72 Square Meters.

To convert from Square Miles to Square Kilometers, use the conversion factor: 1 Square Mile = 2.58999 Square Kilometers.
Formula:
Square Kilometers = Square Miles × 2.58999
Example: 4 Square Miles = 4 × 2.58999 = 10.35996 Square Kilometers.

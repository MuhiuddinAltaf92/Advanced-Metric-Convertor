Definition of Length

Length is a measure of the distance between two points in space. In physical terms, it represents the extent of an object or the space it occupies along a particular dimension. It is typically measured in units like meters (m), centimeters (cm), or feet (ft), depending on the system of measurement (metric or imperial).

Common formulas for calculating length include:

  • Perimeter of a Rectangle: P=2(L+W)
  • Circumference of a Circle: C=2πr
  • Arc Length of a Circle (Sector): L=rθ

Understanding length is essential for applications in fields such as land measurement, construction, and design.

Units of Length

Inches

An inch is a unit of length in the imperial system, equal to 1/12th of a foot or 25.4 millimeters. It's commonly used in the U.S. and UK for measuring short distances. The symbol is in or " (e.g., 12").

Millimeters

A millimeter (mm) is a unit of length in the metric system, equal to 1/1000th of a meter. It is commonly used to measure small distances or dimensions, such as the thickness of materials or tiny objects. The symbol for millimeter is mm.

Feet

A foot (plural: feet) is a unit of length in the imperial and U.S. customary systems, equal to 12 inches or 0.3048 meters. It is commonly used to measure height, length, or short distances, especially in the U.S. The symbol for foot is ft or a single prime ('), as in 5 feet (5').

Meters

A meter (m) is the base unit of length in the metric system, equal to 100 centimeters or 1,000 millimeters. It is commonly used worldwide to measure length or distance, and is defined as the distance light travels in a vacuum in 1/299,792,458 seconds. The symbol for meter is m

Yards

A yard is a unit of length in the imperial and U.S. customary systems, equal to 3 feet or 3 feet. In metric terms, it is equivalent to 0.9144 meters. Yards are often used to measure larger distances in fields such as sports, construction, and fabric. The symbol for yard is yd.

Miles

A mile is a unit of length in the imperial and U.S. customary systems, equal to 5,280 feet or 1,760 yards. In metric terms, one mile is approximately 1.609 kilometers. Miles are commonly used to measure long distances, especially in road travel. The symbol for mile is **mi**.

Kilometers

A kilometer (km) is a unit of length in the metric system, equal to 1,000 meters. It is commonly used to measure longer distances, such as those between cities or for geographic purposes. The symbol for kilometer is km, and it is equivalent to approximately 0.621 miles.
